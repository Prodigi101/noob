About the Author

Matthew Johnson

Entrepreneur, Motivational Speaker & Author

Matthew Johnson is an entrepreneur and motivational thought leader who has built multiple successful businesses from the ground up. As the founder of Mr. Breakfast Jamaica and several other ventures, Matthew understands firsthand what it takes to win in competitive environments.

His journey from conception to achievement—both literally and figuratively—inspired "The Winning Sperm" series. Through his unique perspective combining business acumen with profound life insights, Matthew helps others recognize and activate their natural winning abilities.

Professional Background

  • Founder and operator of Mr. Breakfast Jamaica
  • Successful entrepreneur with multiple business ventures
  • Systems thinker focused on optimization and efficiency
  • Advocate for practical motivation and actionable success strategies

The Winning Sperm Series

"The Winning Sperm" represents Matthew's vision of empowering individuals through a powerful and universal metaphor. The seven-book series takes readers on a comprehensive journey from internal foundation-building to external application, providing both inspiration and practical frameworks for achievement.

Matthew believes that success isn't about becoming someone you're not—it's about remembering who you've always been: a winner.
